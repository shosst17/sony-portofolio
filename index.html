About Me

Hello! I am an enthusiastic Information Technology student at IDE LPKIA. I have a deep interest in modern web development, data management, and network infrastructure. To me, technology is not just about writing code, but a powerful tool to solve real-world problems and improve daily efficiency.

I have professional experience as a Web Developer Intern at PT Solusi Agronusa Pratama. This experience honed my ability to translate business requirements into functional and reliable application features.

To support my professional growth, I have equipped myself with various industry certifications, including CCNA (Networking & Security), Oracle Java Programming, and IT Essentials. I am a lifelong learner ready to contribute to a team, both as a competent Web Developer and IT Support Specialist.
